Best Resources for Marketing Ops Professionals in 2026

Marketing operations is the technical backbone of every marketing team. You own the martech stack, manage data flows, build automations, and make sure campaigns actually reach the right people.

We built this list for MOPs professionals who want to level up without wading through vendor content marketing. Every resource here was recommended by practitioners.

Newsletters

  1. 1. Marrina Decisions Newsletter

    Weekly playbooks for Marketing Ops, RevOps, and MarTech leaders.

  2. 2. MarTech Daily Brief

    Free daily newsletter delivering news and analysis on marketing technology and operations.

  3. 3. The Martech Weekly

    Weekly newsletter and blog covering the marketing technology ecosystem.

Blogs & Websites

  1. 1. MarTech.org (MOPs Section)

    Marketing operations news, trends, and how-to guides from a major MarTech publication.

  2. 2. ChiefMarTec

    Scott Brinker's blog on marketing technology landscape. Publishes the annual MarTech supergraphic.

  3. 3. Martech Zone

    Weekly newsletter digest and publication for researching sales and marketing platforms.

  4. 4. MOPs Report OUR PICK

    Career intelligence and market data for marketing operations professionals.

Communities

  1. 1. MO Pros (MarketingOps.com)

    #1 MOPs community with Slack group, forums, and Pro memberships for marketing ops professionals.

  2. 2. MOPsPROs

    3,400+ member community with monthly MOPsTalks meetings, MOPsCON conference, and MOPsJOBs board.

Courses & Training

  1. 1. HubSpot Academy

    Free marketing automation certifications and courses covering ops-relevant skills.

How We Curated This List

Every resource on this page was evaluated based on editorial independence, content depth, community engagement, and practitioner recommendations. We prioritize sources that provide original analysis over aggregated content.

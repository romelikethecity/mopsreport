Braze Review for Marketing Operations (2026)

Braze is a customer engagement platform built for mobile-first and multi-channel marketing. It is popular with B2C companies and increasingly adopted by B2B organizations with product-led growth motions.

4.1% MOps Job Mention Rate
12 of 295 Postings
2011 Founded
New York, NY Headquarters

Overview

Braze is a customer engagement platform built for mobile-first and multi-channel marketing. It is popular with B2C companies and increasingly adopted by B2B organizations with product-led growth motions.

In our analysis of 295 marketing operations job postings, Braze appears in 12 listings (4.1% of all tracked roles). It represents a notable skill within the marketing automation category.

Key Features

  • Real-time event-triggered messaging across channels
  • Canvas flow builder for journey orchestration
  • Push notifications, in-app messages, email, SMS, and webhooks
  • Currents data streaming for real-time event export
  • Content Cards for persistent in-app content
  • Liquid templating for dynamic personalization

Pricing

Custom pricing based on monthly active users. Typically starts at ~$50K/year for mid-market.

Pricing for Braze varies based on your team size, contact volume, and selected modules. Contact Braze directly for a custom quote based on your requirements.

Pros

  • Best-in-class for mobile and multi-channel engagement
  • Real-time event processing with low latency
  • Strong developer API and SDK ecosystem
  • Growing presence in B2B PLG companies

Cons

  • High entry price excludes smaller companies
  • B2B features lag behind Marketo and HubSpot
  • Requires engineering resources for full implementation
  • Attribution and reporting are less mature than competitors

Who Should Use Braze?

Braze is best suited for MOps teams at mid-market to enterprise organizations that need robust marketing automation capabilities. If you are evaluating Braze, compare it against the alternatives in our Marketing Automation category.

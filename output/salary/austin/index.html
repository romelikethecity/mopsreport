Cost of Living Considerations

Raw salary numbers only tell part of the story. A $127,200 salary in Austin has different purchasing power than the same figure in a lower-cost metro. When evaluating offers, factor in housing costs, state income tax, and commuting expenses.

Some companies headquartered in Austin offer remote positions at adjusted pay bands. Remote MOps roles nationally pay a median of $92,000, compared to $105,000 for onsite positions. See our remote vs. onsite analysis for the full picture.

Negotiation Guidance for Austin

When negotiating a MOps role in Austin, anchor your expectations to the $112,800-$179,825 range. Senior and director-level roles will push above the upper bound. Come prepared with data on the specific tools and skills the role requires, and reference comparable positions in the metro.
